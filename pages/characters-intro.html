"AR-15. Please allow me to formally join this honorable squad and watch me as I bring you victory"

Lightweight and cheap, the civilian's favorite. Goodness this girl takes her job seriously. Confident, strict and grumpy, she usually takes the role of the "straight man" in the team. There isn't a lot to remark about her other than the fact that she's the demolitions expert. So if I were you I wouldn't anger her haha~

Built to be an elite soldier, she despises M4A1 as a leaderno big surpriseand would rather jump in the battle by herself. She's a dangerous one, commander... I like her~

I took this picture while she was spacing out in a surveillance mission, what an elite dork~

"M4 SOPMOD II, finally... Finally... I've found you commander!"

The SPEC-OPS go-to modification kit. Incredibly sadistic and resilient, she's the team's bruiser. I've seen her jump to the enemy lines and tear down dozens of dolls all by herself... You'd expect her to be disobedient but she will always listen to her team's members or commander's orders. She's like an attack dog, but like, super extra lethal.

I don't know whose idea was to give an elite T-doll the need to collect eyes and tear other dolls into pieces... I wonder whether it was intended or not.

Fun fact, taking this picture wasn't an easy task AT ALL. She's too fast, even for a T-Doll!

"Nice to meet you, Commander. RO635 is on standby, awaiting your orders."

While peeking around databases and places I probably shouldn't be I found records mentioning a new member for AR team. I couldn't find a lot regarding this mysterious T-Doll other than her electronic warfare modules are top tier and something about her is "Next generation", don't know what though.

Lucky me, I did manage to get a picture just because I'm that awesome also it's going to cost you extra, she looks fairly unremarkable to me... other than the fact that she looks like a banana with that jacket.
