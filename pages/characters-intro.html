"M4 SOPMOD II, finally... Finally... I've found you commander!"

The SPEC-OPS go-to modification kit. Incredibly sadistic and resilient, she's the team's bruiser. I've seen her jump to the enemy lines and tear down dozens of dolls all by herself... You'd expect her to be disobedient but she will always listen to her team's members or commander's orders. She's like an attack dog, but like, super extra lethal.

I don't know whose idea was to give an elite T-doll the need to collect eyes and tear other dolls into pieces... I wonder whether it was intended or not.

Fun fact, taking this picture wasn't an easy task AT ALL. She's too fast, even for a T-Doll!

"Are you the commander? HK416......Please remember this name, this...extraordinary name."

Lucky for us, she's not the leader here~ Regardless of that, she's one hell of a fighter... which means she has to do most of the heavy lifting here haha.

Holds a massive grudge against M16A1 and the AR team in general because reasons. Also, do NOT give her any alcohol, trust me.

"G11...... Commander, are there enough beds around here?"

The team's marksman and professional sleeper. Despite that, she still gives her best to do the bare minimum and she's pretty good at it... Also the team does need a punching bag after all.

416 is her official caretaker and is always yelling at her or smacking her for being lazy, that's why I always pair them. An "immovable object vs unstoppable force" situation haha~
